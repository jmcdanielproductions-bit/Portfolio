Mobile strategy in three rules

01 — Geometry is desktop-only

The wall's 3D is an enhancement layer, never the content. Mobile renders the same records from the same data as a weighted vertical index. Nothing exists only inside the 3D scene.

02 — Identity travels in color & type

The grade, the slate metadata, the card anatomy, the commentary hand — all CSS, all mobile-safe. A phone visitor experiences the same fiction at a fraction of the GPU cost.

03 — One motion budget

One playing loop, native scroll, no drag physics, no WebGL. Reduced-motion collapses loops to graded stills and the grade to a hard cut — still valid film grammar.
