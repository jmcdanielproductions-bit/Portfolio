Motion system — four verbs, tuned once, used everywhere

01 — Drag to look

The wall pans under an eased gaze. Inertia on release; soft rubber bounds; idle drift returns after 4s of stillness.

lerp 0.075 idle / 0.32 drag · throw ×14 · clamp ±30°
02 — The grade

Two CSS variables re-color the entire environment per world — like a colorist pulling a LUT. Fires on pull, reverses on release.

--mood + --mood-soft · 850ms · sine.inOut · reduced-motion: hard cut
